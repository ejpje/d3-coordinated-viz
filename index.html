Fencing in the United States

Founded in 1891, USA Fencing has developed into a professional organization that supports fencers from novices to Olympic athletes. Modern Olympic-style fencing is built around three weapons: foil, épée, and saber, using electronic equipment to record touches scored in a bout. This map presents a state-by-state visualization of fencing in the United States, including information on: state population, number of USFA-sanctioned (qualified) clubs, active USFA members, tournaments hosted in 2017, and Olympians who attended Rio 2016 and London 2012.
