Fencing in the United States

Founded in 1891, USA Fencing has developed into a professional organization that supports fencers from novices to Olympic athletes. Modern Olympic-style fencing is built around three weapons: foil, épée, and saber, using electronic equipment to record touches scored in a bout. Though still less popular than in other countries, fencing in the United States continues to attract new fans every year.
