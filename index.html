Fencing in the United States

Founded in 1891, USA Fencing has developed into a professional organization that supports a wide range of competitors, from novices to Olympic athletes. Modern Olympic-style fencing is built around three weapons: foil, épée, and saber. The number of local clubs and university-sponsored teams continues to increase, reflecting the steady expansion of the fencing fanbase.
