Fencing in the United States

Modern Olympic-style fencing is built around three weapons: foil, épée, and saber, and uses electronic equipment to keep track of points. Fencing in the United States is organized by the United States Fencing Association (USFA), and includes fencers with skills ranging from novices to Olympic athletes.
This map presents a state-by-state visualization of fencing in the United States, including information on: state population, number of USFA-sanctioned (qualified) clubs, active USFA members, tournaments hosted in 2017, and Olympians who attended the Rio 2016 or the London 2012 Olympics.

Want to learn more? Check out USA Fencing's Learn To Fence page, this overview by Fencing.net, or the Wikipedia page!
